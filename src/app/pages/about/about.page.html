About

First Aid and Traditional Medication App teaches ways of using natural remedies and knowledge of basic first aid. From easy-to-find herbs to quick tips for common health issues.

Explore the world of traditional medication with confidence as we guide you through its principles, enriching your knowledge about natural healing methods. Gain valuable insights into managing common illnesses and acquire essential first aid skills to handle emergencies effectively.
